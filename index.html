WenXin Dong

Hi! Welcome to my little corner of the internet. My name is WenXin Dong. I'm an undergrad at Stanford University studying Computer Science with a concentration in AI. Being a CS student, I love using technology to solve practical problems. In the past, I have published a Python book and co-founded a start up project named Club Cardinal. More recently, I have been conducting AI research in the areas of affective content analysis, reinforcement learning, and the interpretability of deep learning. I am a research assistant at Stanford Center for Ocean Solutions, and I have been an undergraduate researcher at Professor Noah Goodman and Professor Leonidas Guibas lab. In my free time, I like to draw and paint :) Check out my research and side projects!
